Community Guidelines

Keep Student Hustle Hub safe and helpful for everyone.

Do

  • Be honest about pricing and skills.
  • Communicate clearly and on time.
  • Respect privacy and boundaries.
  • Leave fair, constructive reviews.

Do not

  • Post scams, fake services, or plagiarism.
  • Harass or pressure other students.
  • Share private data publicly.
  • Abuse the reporting or review system.
